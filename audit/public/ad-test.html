DeFi TVL Surges 23% as New Protocols Launch on Base

The total value locked across DeFi protocols has seen a significant increase this quarter, driven by the launch of several new protocols on Base network. Analysts attribute the growth to improved user experience and lower transaction costs.

Leading the charge are several innovative lending and yield farming protocols that have attracted both retail and institutional capital. The trend suggests a renewed interest in on-chain financial products.

"We're seeing a new wave of DeFi adoption that's fundamentally different from 2021," said one industry analyst. "The infrastructure is more mature, the user experience is better, and the yields are more sustainable."

Market observers note that the growth in TVL has been accompanied by an increase in unique active wallets, suggesting genuine user adoption rather than capital recycling.
